Detailed Dashboard

TalentInsightAI provides HR personnel with a comprehensive dashboard to visualize and analyze candidate data. The dashboard displays key metrics such as candidate demographics, qualifications, experience, skills proficiency, and cultural fit scores. HR personnel can customize the dashboard to focus on specific criteria or job roles and track the progress of candidates throughout the hiring process.
